\begin{abstract}
Computational experiments for the \kctp : studying the performence of existing strategies (\textsc{greedy}, \textsc{comparison}, \textsc{reposition} and \dichorep) on instances modeling urban networks. We also evaluate $\gamma$ for groups of instances.
\end{abstract}
